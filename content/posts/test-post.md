## Why Every Living Room Needs an Accent Chair

An accent chair is one of the easiest ways to add personality to your living room. Whether you want a bold pop of colour or a subtle texture upgrade, the right chair pulls the whole room together.

## Our Top 5 Picks

Here are the best accent chairs for every budget.

### 1. Rivet Revolve Modern Accent Chair

This mid-century inspired chair is our top pick for most living rooms. Clean lines, sturdy frame, and it comes in over 20 colours.

### 2. Stone and Beam Bradbury Chair

For those who want luxury, the Bradbury delivers hotel-quality comfort at a fraction of designer prices.

### 3. Amazon Basics Classic Accent Chair

The best budget pick under $150. Simple, solid, and ships in two days.

## How to Choose the Right Accent Chair

Consider three things: scale, fabric, and function. A chair that is too large will dominate the room. A delicate fabric will not survive kids or pets. Always ask if the chair will actually get used or is just decorative.

## Final Thoughts

Any of these five chairs will upgrade your living room instantly. Start with your budget, then match to your existing colour palette.
